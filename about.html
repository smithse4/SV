SmithVentures was founded by Stephanie and Sallie Smith in 2021 out of their love of the arts. It is made up of three divisions: SmithVenturePrints - an art print Etsy shop, Sallie Ritter Smith Art - a fine art shop, and Hey, let’s make something! - a crafty blog. Between the three branches, SmithVentures touches a spectrum of the creative arts.
